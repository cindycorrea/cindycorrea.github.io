Tours

You are free to explore the island however you want. Here are a few suggestions of routes to travel:

Isla de Pasion

Make your way out to this local beach where you can enjoy this small peninsula with white-sand beach, water hammocks & activities like kayaking & paddle boarding. Start at Playa del Carmen Ferry Dock and travel North around the island. Full day rental recommended.

Discover Mexico Park Cozumel

Scooter your way down to this little piece of culture. Here you can enjoy Mexican culture theme park style with detailed models, videos & experiences like chocolate and soda tasting.

Coral Reefs Monument

Just a hop, skip, and a jump from our Playa del Carmen Ferry Dock location, you can't miss seeing this unique monument. Great for photo backdrop for some of the most amazing photos you will take on your trip.

South Coast Tour

Get away from the hustle and bustle of the main city and enjoy the quiet and peace of nature. The South trail will refill you and leave you relaxed and ready for some exciting night life.
