I Love Soda Springs!

Chloe Carter, 9-year-old from Denver, recently visited Soda Springs with her family. They visited many of the small towns in the area, including Fish Haven and Preston. "Our family has always wanted to take a trip to Southern Idaho" said Chloe's mom, "We just could never find time before, but boy are we glad we did! This place is beautiful."

They've spent the last 10 days in the area, getting to know the people, visiting the sites, hiking, and enjoying the local cuisine. "I've never tasted so many different forms of roadkill before!" Chloe's dad said, "It's so interesting to see how these people bring in what they hit with their cars and turn it into something really special and surprisingly edible!"

Chloe reflects on her visit, "I like coming here to visit. I'm glad that my mom and dad brought us here. I don't want to live her though. I would miss of of my friends. I do want to come back and visit!"
